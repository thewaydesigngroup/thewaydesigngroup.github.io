What is Urban Design?

You may see Urban Design (UD) used a lot on this website. That is because that is what we primarily do. UD is an area of practice that is sandwiched between City/Urban Planning and Architecture. It serves as a critical link across disciplines in the built environment with the value of engaging stakeholders and clients early in the development process to give vision, form, metrics and pathways for development.
